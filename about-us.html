About the Owner



Haji Niaz Ahmed, the respectable owner of “Niaz Ahmed and Brothers”, started his business in 1985 from scratch. While he still managed to expand his business far and wide with his determination and hardwork. Initially, he was a farmer and has his own fields and lands for which he bought DAP and urea, but unluckily the products were foist mixture which actually ruined the fields by time. Thereafter, in 1985 he started his own business of fertilisers, pesticides, seeds and cement on recommendation of his brother .

The first company to join hands with was NFC, the agency of fertilisers. Followed by Angro fertilisers in 2002 , after which the trade from different companies increased and the number of partnerships with renowned companies increased gradually.
